Project NULA

Project Nula was part of an art installation called "Intercelluar". This light projection art was projected onto the Sydney Quandrangle back in 2015 as part of a week-long light celebration. "Intercelluar" is a series of short films created by staff and students in partnership with Indigeous artists from Eora Tafe, the Sydney Conservatorium of Music and the Faculty of Science. The Vivid light show was held from the 25th of May to the 31st. Nula was awarded 1st prize and was featured in the Sydney Morning Herald.

House Quest

House Quest is an Artificial Intelligent app that allows parents and children monitor screen time usage and set goals. Simply, connect all the smart devices like TVs, laptops, phones in homes and House Quest can collect data. Artificial intelligence automatically can detect whether the user is working or playing. Through gamification techniques this app helps adjust behaviours for children and adults.

1, 2, Pack!

1, 2, Pack! is an award winning concept from a Woolworths competition. This idea has won the People's Choice Award in 2017. This lunchbox idea allows stressed and tired parents have a break from packing daily school lunches for children. Children can go with their parents on their shopping trips and build their own lunches. They can pick out their own main meal such as a sandwich, fruit/veg and a dessert.All portions are carefully measured to ensure the children can have a nutritous morning tea, and lunch. These lunchboxes are available at metro stores, Caltex petrol stores and general stores.
